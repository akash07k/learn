# Subject Template

The ubiquitous language for this subject: a glossary of roles and artifact vocabulary only, no
implementation details. Replace this stub with the real roles and terms your subject uses, and keep
every guide faithful to them.

## Language

### Roles

**Operator**: The person following the guides. Define who they are, what machine they work from, and
what access they have. State the words to _avoid_ for this role.

### Artifacts

**Guide**: A numbered, self-contained Markdown document under `guides/` teaching one area in reading
order. The backbone of the subject. _Avoid_: tutorial, chapter, lesson.
